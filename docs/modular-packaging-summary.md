# Tenvo Modular Packaging System - Summary

## Overview

A new **6-tier pricing structure** with **modular packaging** allowing businesses to pick specific functional areas or choose bundled tiers. Optimized for Pakistan market with aggressive pricing to compete with Zoho.

## Tier-by-Tier Feature Breakdown

**For**: Solo entrepreneurs, freelancers testing the platform

**Includes**:
- 100 customers, 100 products, 50 vendors
- Unlimited invoicing (100/month)
- Basic accounting & reports
- 100MB storage

**Zoho Comparison**: More generous than Zoho Free (which has revenue limits)

## Tenvo vs Zoho - Feature Advantages

### 1. Better Value Pricing
- Tenvo Growth (₹2,499) = Zoho Professional (₹12,000) at **79% less cost**
- No per-user charges within tier limits
- More generous free tier (100 customers vs Zoho's revenue cap)

### 2. Features Zoho Doesn't Have
- **Offline POS Mode** - Continue selling without internet
- **Pakistani Tax Compliance** - Built-in FBR integration
- **Urdu Language Support** - Full UI in Urdu
- **Biometric Attendance** - Fingerprint/face recognition
- **Kitchen Display System** - Included in Growth (Zoho: separate product)
- **SMS Gateway Integration** - Local Pakistani providers

### 3. Earlier Access to Premium Features
- **API Access**: Starter tier (Zoho: Standard/Professional)
- **Shopify Integration**: Growth tier (Zoho: Elite only)
- **Multi-currency**: Growth tier (Zoho: Professional+)
- **KDS**: Growth tier (Zoho: Separate expensive product)

### 4. Local Support
- Urdu-speaking support team
- Pakistan business hours
- Local phone support
- On-site training available
